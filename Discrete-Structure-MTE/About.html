About Splitzi

Our aim is to develop a platform based on an algorithm which can restructure cash transactions within groups of people. The algorithm does not aim to change the total amount that any of the participants owes, but it makes it easier to pay people back by minimizing the total number of transactions.

In technical terms we can say that, given a Directed Graph representing cash transactions, we have to change (if needed) the weights on the existing edges without introducing newer ones.
